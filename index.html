Mom's Okay Spaghetti

It's not horrible™️

This is my mom's famous spaghetti. It is not authentic and some might
argue that it is not "good". But you know what? It's Not Horrible™️. And
that's on Chef Boyardee. Boy, this stuff is alright.


Ingredients:

  • 1 Gallon of ketchup
  • Hot Dogs
  • Noodles (any kind is alright)
  • Hot Cheetos

Directions

  1. First off, invite your friends, Mom's making her Okay Spaghetti after all.
    • That means Giovanni, Tony, Mario, Alfredo, get over here, 7pm sharp.
  2. Okay so get comfortable
  3. Get a nice big pot and fill it with water, boil it over any source of heat

Making the Spaghetti Sauce

  1. It's actually ketchup and store bought is fine

Directions Part 2: This Time, It's Personal

  1. cut your weenies in any shape and stick the solid noodles through the weenie
  2. repeat until you've made your desired amount of meat noodles
  3. At this point, your water should be boiling, if not boil it again
  4. drop the weenie noodles in the pot
